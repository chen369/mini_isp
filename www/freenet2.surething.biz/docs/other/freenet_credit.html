FreeNet.surething.biz Franchise credit offer


If you don't have the money needed to start your FreeNet franchise you still have some hope left. FreeNet can offer the money needed to some groups on credit/share holder terms to start your franchise if FreeNet sees a good possibility of payback in the location you plan to install your system. In some cases we will finance 100% of the total startup cost but normally would like some token from the franchise client to show true interest in the plan or provide some existing assets needed for the network. First give us a call and tell us the location and the size of the building you plan to install a FreeNet network. If the size and location meet to our standards we will come out and take a closer look at the location and talk with the needed parties to setup a possible installation or test site. In the event we do agree to finance these are the terms we required.


In this example case FreeNet is a part lender and part share holder in you franchise for example in a 100% finance we become a 49% share holder in your franchise. We are entitled 49% of the profits made by your franchise site. The profit is calculated after payment of all fee's to the ISP providers possible maintenance and misc expenses and the 15% gross franchise license fee's that are still payable to FreeNet (see license agreement statement to see details of license fee's). After a test period of normally 3 months to 1 year FreeNet will give you the client the option to buy out our shares of the franchise. If you the client are not interested in buying out the FreeNets shares at the offered price determined by what we see the value is at that time, then FreeNet has the option to offer to buy your 51% shares. If both parties find the business to be none profitable and NOT wish to continue at the offered prices then FreeNet can exercise it's right to foreclose on all purchased and licensed assets held by the franchise including servers (if purchased with FreeNet capital), software, network switches, access points, network equipment, wiring and terminate the ISP services and all other parts setup with FreeNet capital. There is always the possibility that both parties decide to continue to hold the 49% and 51% shares over longer term but most franchise holders decide to buy out some or all shares when the money becomes available. FreeNet normally offers to sell there shares at a reasonable price to pay costs and expenses with about 20% profit, so we can take the money to finance another new site. The more site we have the more we all make.

Freenet.surething.biz rights

1. When you the franchise client run the install script as root on the desired server, FreeNet has the right to change delete or add any and all files on that server to make it function to the best of it's ability as a FreeNet network server.


2. FreeNet has the right to create a user account on the server with the name: FreeNet that will have superuser privileges with a home directory at /home/freenet.


3. FreeNet has the right to remotely monitor and log network traffic and view any and all files on the server that are needed to function and test the network and to provide accounting of usage of the network. Note remote monitoring will be done with VPN (virtual protected network) tunnel from our remote central site.


4. FreeNet has the right to refuse service and discontinue operations on the server for any reason. This could be for not paying the agreed fee payments or for not operating at FreeNet minimal specifications or for any other reason FreeNet sees fit.


5. FreeNet has the right to provide roaming customers up to 2 days of free service in a single month on your network system. When a roaming customer makes use of a your system for more than 2 days in one month, your network will receive part of that months income. If the customer starts to make more than 50% of a single months operating days on your network they will be transfered to you as the local server and told to provide payment by the methods you the franchise client desire.


6. FreeNet has the right to provide new customers 2 days free trial to try before they buy and to setup a gap of time to collect payment for services without delayed installation. FreeNet software makes sure that customers don't take advantage of this prolonged usage of free service. FreeNet is constantly looking for new freeloading methods and will prevent it before it happens.

1. The client may continue to use the server as a normal user console if desired, but must continue to run the Ubuntu operating system needed for FreeNet to operate. 2. The client has the right to discontinue operating as part of the FreeNet franchise at any time. Simply contact FreeNet and we will uninstall the FreeNet software for you or instruct you on how to do so. At this point accounts will be settled and closed. 3. The client has the right to audit the accounting of customers and view logs of network traffic at any time. The client will be given access to the needed log files and be given instruction on there locations to view network traffic and user payments 24 hours a day 7 days a week.


4. The client has the right to monitor network traffic to verify proper function of the network and for auditing purposes.

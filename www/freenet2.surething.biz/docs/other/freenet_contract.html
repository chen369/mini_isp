FreeNet.surething.biz Franchise Agreement contract


The FreeNet Franchise client must agree to the following before they download and install FreeNet software on a server. Running the install script on any server is equivalent to signing this agreement.


FreeNet agrees to:

1. FreeNet will Provide the use of all the needed software to conduct business with the FreeNet logo from standard Ethernet and Wifi and possibly other methods of network communication, that will provide your customers with Internet usage and you with customer tracking and billing to collect fees from customers for Internet services that you will provide.


2. FreeNet will Provide the needed training to the franchise client to enable them to monitor system usage and collect needed fee's from customers.


3. FreeNet will provide prepaid clients with up to 7 service phone calls or remote sessions calls per month for no more than 5 billable hours per month. Prepaid clients must provide a minimum 1000 baht prepaid credit to FreeNet before service is rendered.


Freenet.surething.biz rights

1. When you the franchise client run the install script as root on the desired server, FreeNet has the right to change delete or add any and all files on that server to make it function to the best of it's ability as a FreeNet network server.


2. FreeNet has the right to create a user account on the server with the name: FreeNet that will have superuser privileges with a home directory at /home/freenet.


3. FreeNet has the right to remotely monitor and log network traffic and view any and all files on the server that are needed to function and test the network and to provide accounting of usage of the network. Note remote monitoring will be done with VPN (virtual protected network) tunnel from our remote central site.


4. FreeNet has the right to refuse service and discontinue operations on the server for any reason. This could be for not paying the agreed fee payments or for not operating at FreeNet minimal specifications or for any other reason FreeNet sees fit.


5. FreeNet has the right to provide roaming customers up to 2 days of free service in a single month on your network system. When a roaming customer makes use of a your system for more than 2 days in one month, your network will receive part of that months income. If the customer starts to make more than 50% of a single months operating days on your network they will be transfered to you as the local server and told to provide payment by the methods you the franchise client desire.


6. FreeNet has the right to provide new customers 2 days free trial to try before they buy and to setup a gap of time to collect payment for services without delayed installation. FreeNet software makes sure that customers don't take advantage of this prolonged usage of free service. FreeNet is constantly looking for new freeloading methods and will prevent it before it happens.


FreeNet Franchise Clients rights

1. The client may continue to use the server as a normal user console if desired, but must continue to run the Ubuntu operating system needed for FreeNet to operate. FreeNet software takes very little power or memory to operate and will not be noticed by a user at the console of the server.


2. The client has the right to discontinue operating as part of the FreeNet franchise at any time. Simply contact FreeNet and we will uninstall the FreeNet software for you or instruct you on how to do so. At this point accounts will be settled and closed. FreeNet includes encrypted SSL key sets that will expire if not updated by FreeNet and will render the server useless even if the software is not removed but will continue to use system resources if not removed.


3. The client has the right to audit the accounting of customers and view logs of network traffic at any time. The client will be given access to the needed log files and be given instruction on there locations to view network traffic and user payments 24 hours a day 7 days a week.


4. The client has the right to monitor network traffic to verify proper function of the network and for auditing purposes.


FreeNet Client agrees to:

1. Client agrees to Pay 15% of the gross earnings of the network when operated with more than 4 steady monthly users. This means you have received income from 4+ customers in a single month as tracked by the FreeNet software and optionally audited by the client or other party's. Payments will be made to FreeNet monthly unless agreed upon by other custom terms. Payments can be made in cash or paypal or direct deposit to our Thailand bank account. If payments are made to paypal or bank deposit please email us to verify we should be expecting to receive your payment to verify and credit your account. 4 or less customers in any monthly billing cycle will require NO fee's paid to FreeNet unless other separate services were received from FreeNet. Included services for prepaid clients include up to 7 customer service phone calls per month or remote console training/fixes. These calls can be from the franchise client or his/her customers. Other services not included in standard franchise fee's include on site and class training, some engineering on larger projects (most standard engineering is included), On site repair, Contracted Installations of room to room wiring. If FreeNet finds there software or hardware installation at fault the client will not be charged.


2. Client agrees to NOT make any changes to files in the /home/freenet account files or to any files in /etc, /var directories without first informing FreeNet. FreeNet is open for change but we just need to know what you are doing so that we can be a part of it to try to maintain our standards of operation. The installed files md5 checksum is recorded before installation to detect if any changes by the client have been made. Any changes by the client in the /home/freenet directory without FreeNet consent will be considered breach of contract and could lead to instant discontinued services from FreeNet.


3. Client agrees to NOT duplicate or reverse engineer the FreeNet software or hardware configurations to be used outside of the Franchise system without FreeNet.surething.biz consent.


4. Client agrees to NOT create back doors to enable customers to bypass the FreeNet accounting system.


5. Client agrees to maintain the server and FreeNet network system to meet FreeNet minimal specifications. See software installation documentation for minimal system requirements. FreeNet will also remotely monitor and inform the franchise client when the system no longer meets our standards.
